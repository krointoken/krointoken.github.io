INTRODUCTION

Beginning
Kroin token was created on July 2, 2021. Its main purpose is to create a website for freelancers to work on. The initial target of token creation is investment for holders by implementing profit sharing on Kroin Tokens.

Problems and Handling
Some will think that the rise and fall of prices will be an obstacle in payment. For example, Person A pays freelancers for 100 in dollars, then exchanges them for Kroin tokens for 2$, a week later the price of Kroin tokens drops to 1$, meaning the value goes down and workers will get only 50 in dollars, different from the initial agreement which was 100 in dollars . But wait, we will not make anyone lose, therefore, if the website is formed later, we will use FIAT currency as the basis for payment.

Kroinomics
This token cuts costs for initial website creation, details of costs will be shared later. Our plan, the website will be created after the market capital reaches 1 million dollars.

Prospect Kroin Token
The initial goal is to complete the whitelist, blockfolio, coingenko, and coinmarketcap listings. We will also try to enter various exchanges in the future.

Kroin Token Investment Development Project
The name of this website our plan is kroinlancer. As on freelancer websites in general, there will be recruiters and workers. The plan is that this website will only cut transactions received by 5% from recruiters and 5% from workers. This profit in fiat currency will later be placed in 1 account which will later be purchased in Kroin Tokens entered every week with the provisions of 80% for holders and 20% for managers.

Burn and Mint System
We try to see the development of this burn and mint system as needed in the future.
